Systemic by Layal Liverpool

Layal's debut book Systemic is an investigation of the shocking and insidious ways in which racism harms health — and an exploration of how we can tackle the problem to create a fairer and healthier world. It has been translated into German and Dutch, and was shortlisted for the New York Public Library's 2025 Helen Bernstein Book Award for Excellence in Journalism. Systemic is out now.
