Press & interviews

Layal is a recognised expert in biomedical science and health; she has been interviewed by and had her work featured in major international news outlets, and she has appeared on radio, podcasts, TV and social media broadcasts internationally. Layal has also delivered talks and participated in discussion panels at world-leading universities, such as Oxford and Princeton, as well as at prominent conferences and festivals, including the Frankfurt Book Fair and the Hay Festival.
