Selected works

In 2021, Layal was shortlisted for a British Journalism Award based on her health and life sciences reporting for New Scientist. She has also been shortlisted for awards by the Medical Journalists' Association and the Association of British Science Writers, and she was the recipient of a British Science Association Media Fellowship in 2018.
